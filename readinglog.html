Pride and Prejudice

Status: Finished

1984

Author: George Orwell

Status: In Progress

Notes: The dystopian world is intense but very thought-provoking. I like how Orwell explains the dangers of totalitarianism in simple language.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

Status: Finished

The Lottery

Status: Finished
