Featured Authors

Learn about famous authors who have created unforgettable stories. Each author has a unique style and has contributed something special to literature.

Jane Austen

Jane Austen wrote stories like Pride and Prejudice and Emma. Her books explore love, family, and society, often with humor and clever observations. She shows the challenges of social rules in her time.

George Orwell

George Orwell wrote 1984 and Animal Farm. His stories focus on politics, fairness, and society. He warns about corruption, government control, and the importance of truth and freedom.

Harper Lee

Harper Lee wrote To Kill a Mockingbird. Her story teaches about justice, kindness, and understanding others. It shows life in the American South and the challenges of fairness and equality.

J.K. Rowling

J.K. Rowling created the Harry Potter series. Her books bring magical adventures, friendship, and courage to readers of all ages. She shows how characters grow and overcome challenges together.
