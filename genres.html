What is a literary genre?

A genre is a category that groups stories together based on their style, subject, or theme. Understanding genres makes it easier to find books you will enjoy, and helps readers explore different types of stories from adventure to mystery or romance.

Fantasy

Fantasy stories are filled with magic, mythical creatures, and imaginative worlds. They often involve heroes on quests, magical powers, and adventures that take you far from the real world.

Mystery

Mystery stories keep you guessing with puzzles, secrets, and investigations. The main characters usually work to solve a crime or uncover hidden truths, leading to a surprising or satisfying ending.

Romance

Romance stories focus on love and relationships. They explore emotions, personal growth, and how people connect with each other, often ending in a happy or meaningful conclusion.

Science Fiction

Science fiction imagines the future, space travel, advanced technology, and alternate realities. These stories explore “what if” ideas and often ask big questions about humanity and society.

Historical Fiction

Historical fiction mixes real historical events with imagined characters and stories. It gives readers a glimpse into past cultures, important moments, and daily life from different time periods.

Horror

Horror stories aim to scare, surprise, or unsettle the reader. They often include monsters, ghosts, or other frightening situations, and explore fear in a safe, thrilling way.
